What is the  SHARE Network?

The SHARE Network is a  community centered space dedicated to supporting Black mothers, people of color and their families through their maternal mental health journey. Here, you'll find a wealth of resources, including real-life stories, educational tools, and expert guidance, all designed to be accessible and culturally relevant.

  • Access comprehensive mental health resources tailored for Black mothers and people of color
  • Engage with empowering stories and expert insights
  • Discover tools and guides to support your mental health journey
  • Connect with a community that understands and supports you
ABOUT

Shades of
Blue Project

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap in maternal mental health care for Black mothers and families, providing culturally sensitive resources and support to empower their mental health journey before, during and after pregnancy.

Background

The Shades of Blue Project was founded to address the disparities in maternal mental health care for BIPOC communities, ensuring that every mother receives the respect, support, and resources they deserve.

FRAMEWORK

SHARE Framework

Our SHARE framework is at the heart of everything we do, focusing on Support, Healing, Advocacy, Respect, and Empowerment to enhance maternal mental health.

Support

Establishing robust support systems for maternal mental health, ensuring access to the resources and assistance that is needed.

Healing

Focusing on the healing journey particularly within BIPOC communities, by offering culturally responsive care and resources designed to foster recovery, resilience, and overall well-being.

Advocate

Advocating for the needs and rights through community-centered initiatives, raising awareness, and driving systemic change to improve maternal mental health outcomes.

Respect

Ensuring respect for all cultural backgrounds and experiences. This means acknowledging and valuing the diverse experiences and tailoring support to meet their unique needs.
